Witchy Design System

The Witchy Design System (WDS) is essentially a Bootstrap v5.2 fork that I'm customizing to suit my needs and aesthetic preferences.

My main needs for WDS are: simple design, good mobile support, and great components for academic content like (end/foot/side)notes, citations and glossaries.

Additionally, I need components for some linguistic oddities like ruby annotations, diffs, parallel texts and interlinear text.
